Crime Books Authors

Agatha Christie

an English writer known for her sixty-six detective novels and fourteen short story collections.

Peter James

a British writer of crime fiction. He was born in Brighton, the son of Cornelia James, the former glovemaker to Queen Elizabeth II.

James Patterson

an American author and philanthropist. Among his works are the Alex Cross, Michael Bennett and Women's Murder Club.

Lee Child

a British author who writes thriller novels, and is best known for his Jack Reacher novel series.
