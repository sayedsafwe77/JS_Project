Comics Books Authors

Alan Moore

Alan Moore is an English writer most famous for his influential work in comics, including the acclaimed graphic novels Watchmen.

Brian K. Vaughan

is the writer and co-creator of comic-book series including SAGA, PAPER GIRLS, Y THE LAST MAN, RUNAWAYS, and most recently, BARRIER.

Frank Miller

is an American writer, artist and film director best known for his film noir-style comic book stories.

Robert Kirkman

is an American comic book writer best known for his work on The Walking Dead as well as Ultimate X-Men and Marvel Zombies for Marvel Comics.
